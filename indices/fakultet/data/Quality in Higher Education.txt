Quality in Higher Education

Commission for Quality Enhancement and Assurance in Higher Education and Office of Quality Enhancement and Assurance in Higher Education are responsible for quality assurance and enhancement at the Department of Mathematics. They cooperate with the Committee for Quality Enhancement and Assurance in Higher Education at Josip Juraj Strossmayer University of Osijek and the Centre for Quality Enhancement and Assurance in Higher Education at Josip Juraj Strossmayer University of Osijek, the Agency for Science and Higher Education, as well as internal and external stakeholders in the quality assurance system of the Department of Mathematics.
